# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Isaac Newton]] Third Law)[^1]
> When one body exerts [[Force]] on the other, the second exerts an equal in magnitude, but opposite in direction [[Force]] on the first ($\mathbf{F}_{AB} = - \mathbf{F}_{BA}$).
> Each action has an equal and opposite reaction.

[^1]: [Physics, Volume 1](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/5QBK4P96?page=66)
