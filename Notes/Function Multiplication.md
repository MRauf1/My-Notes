# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Function Multiplication)
> For [[Function|functions]] $f, g$, with $x$ in the [[Domain|domain]] of both $f$ and $g$, the product is[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> (f \cdot g)(x) = f(x) \cdot g(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
> The [[Domain]] is $X_1 \cap X_2$
