# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Trinomial)[^1]
> $\forall x_1, a_1, b_1, x_2, a_2, b_2, x_3, a_3, b_3$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> a_1 x_1^{b_1} + a_2 x_2^{b_2} + a_3 x_3^{b_3}
> \end{align}
> $$

Trinomial is a [[Polynomial|Polynomial]] with $3$ [[Term|terms]].
