# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Cotangent Function]])[^1]
> If $f(x) = \cot(x)$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(\cot(x)) = - \csc^2(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
