# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Perfect [[Subset]])[^1]
> [[Closed Set]] [[Subset]] $E$ of [[Metric Space]] $(S, d)$ is perfect if every [[Point]] $x$ in $E$ is a [[Limit Point]] of a [[Sequence]] of [[Point]] in $E \setminus \{x\}$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=185)
