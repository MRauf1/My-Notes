# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Set Union)
> For [[Set|sets]] $A, B$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> A \cup B = \{x | x \in A \lor x \in B \}
> \end{align}
> $$

Union of two sets includes elements that are in either set.[^1]

> [!info] Definition 2 ([[Collection of Set]] Union)[^2]
> For [[Collection of Set]] $\mathcal{F}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \cup \mathcal{F} = \{x | \exists A \in \mathcal{F}, x \in A\}
> \end{align}
> $$
> If $\mathcal{F} = \emptyset$, then $\cup \mathcal{F} = \emptyset$
