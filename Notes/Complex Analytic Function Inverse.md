# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Analytic Function]] [[Inverse Function]])
> Let $U \subseteq \mathbb{C}$ be [[Open Set]] and $f: U \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be [[Analytic Function]]. Then we can define $f^{-1}(z)$ as a [[Multi-Valued Function]] $f^{-1}(z) := \{u \in U | f(u) = z\}$. Let $g$ be [[Branch]] of $f^{-1}(z)$ on [[Domain]] $D$ and take $z_0 \in D$. If $f'(g(z_0)) \neq 0$, then $g$ is [[Differentiable Function]] at $z_0$ and $g'(z_0) = \frac{1}{f'(g(z_0))}$. Moreover, if $f' \neq 0$ on $g(D)$, then $g$ is [[Analytic Function]] on $D$.
