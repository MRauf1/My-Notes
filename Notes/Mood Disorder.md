# Definition

Severe disturbances in mood and [[Emotion]].[^1]

# Types
- [[Depressive Disorder]]
- [[Bipolar and Related Disorder]]

# Causes
- Strong genetic component
- Imbalances with [[Neurotransmitter]], such as [[Norepinephrine]] and [[Serotonin]]
