# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Relative Risk)
> For measuring [[Homogeneity]], the relative risk is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> RR = \frac{p_1}{p_2}
> \end{align}
> $$
> If $RR = 1$, then we have homogeneity.
