# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Contour Riemann Integral]] [[Absolute Value of Complex Number]] [[Inequality]])
> Let $f: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be [[Continuous Function]] and let $\gamma: [a, b] \rightarrow D$ be a [[Piecewise Smooth Curve]]. Then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> |\int_{\gamma} f(z) dz| \leq \max_{z \in |\gamma|} |f(z)| \cdot \text{length}(\gamma)
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $|\gamma|$ is the [[Image]] of the [[Curve]].
