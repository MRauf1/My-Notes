# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Parallel [[Plane]])[^1]
> Two planes are parallel if their [[Normal Vector]] are [[Parallel Vector]].
> If they are not parallel, then they intersect in a [[Line]] with the angle between the two planes defined as the [[Acute Angle]] between their [[Normal Vector]].

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=860)
