# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Cross [[Covariance]])
> For two [[Stochastic Process]] $X_t, Y_t$, their cross covariance is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \gamma_{xy}(h) = Cov[X_{t + h}, Y_t]
> \end{align}
> $$
