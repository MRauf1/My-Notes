# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Partition)[^1]
> Partition of [[Interval Notation]] $[a, b]$ is any finite ordered [[Subset]] $P$ of the form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P = \{a = t_0 < t_1 < \dots < t_n = b\}
> \end{align}
> $$
