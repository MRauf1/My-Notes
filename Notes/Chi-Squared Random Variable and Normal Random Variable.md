# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Relation between [[Chi-Squared Distribution]] and [[Normal Distribution]])[^1]
> Let $Z ~ N(0, 1)$ and $Z_1, \dots, Z_v ~ N(0, 1)$. Then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Z^2 &= \chi^2(1) \\
> Z_1^2 + \dots + Z_v^2 &= \chi^2(v)
> \end{align}
> $$
