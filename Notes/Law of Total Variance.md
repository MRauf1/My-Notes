# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Law of Total [[Variance]])
> The variance of a  [[Random Variable]] $X$ in relation to its [[Conditional Expectation]] and [[Conditional Variance]] with respect to a RV $Y$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[X] = E[Var[X | Y]] + Var[E[X | Y]]
> \end{align}
> $$
