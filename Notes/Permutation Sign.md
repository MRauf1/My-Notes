# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Permutation Sign)
> Function $sign: S_n \rightarrow \{\pm 1\}$ such that
> 1) $sign(e) = 1$
> 2) $sign(\tau) = -1$, where $\tau$ is a [[Transposition]]
> 3) $sign(\sigma \circ \tau) = sign(\sigma) \cdot sign(\tau)$, where $\sigma, \tau \in S_n$
