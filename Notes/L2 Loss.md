# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (L2 Loss)
> $$
> \begin{align}
> L(\hat{y}, y) = (\hat{y} - y)^2
> \end{align}
> $$

L2 loss is the [[Exponentiation|squared]] [[Subtraction|difference]] between the [[Variable|variables]].[^1]

[^1]: https://visionbook.mit.edu/intro_to_learning.html
