# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Joint [[Probability Distribution]])
> For [[Random Variable]] $X, Y$, their joint probability distribution expressed by the joint density is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P(X \cap Y) = P(X = x, Y = y)\ \text{(Discrete)} \\
> f(x, y)\ \text{(Continuous)}
> \end{align}
> $$
