# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Indefinite [[Integral]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int f(x) dx = F(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $F'(x) = f(x)$ ($F$ is the [[Antiderivative]] of $f$)

It is a [[Function]].
