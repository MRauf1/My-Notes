# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Divides)
> For $x, y \in \mathbb{Z}$, $x | y$ ($x$ divides $y$) if[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \exists k \in \mathbb{Z}, kx = y
> \end{align}
> $$

$x$ divides $y$ if $y$ is [[Divisible|divisible]] by $x$. Alternatively, $x$ is a [[Factor]]/divisor of $y$, or $y$ is a [[Multiple]] of $x$.
