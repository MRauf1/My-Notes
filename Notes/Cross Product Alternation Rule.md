# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Cross Product]] Alternation Rule)[^1]
> For [[Vector]] $\mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w} \in \mathbb{R}^n$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{w} = - \mathbf{w} \times \mathbf{v}
> \end{align}
> $$
> This implies that $\mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{v} = 0$.
