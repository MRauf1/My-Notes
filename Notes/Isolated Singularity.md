# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Isolated Singularity)
> $z_0 \in \mathbb{C}$ is an isolated singularity of a [[Function]] $f$ if $f$ is [[Complex Analytic Function]] on the [[Punctured Open Disk]] $B^*(z_0, r)$ for some $r > 0$.
