# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Conjugate)
> For a [[Group]] element $x \in G$, the conjugation [[Function]] by for some $g \in G$, is $c_g: G \rightarrow G$ with $c_g(x) := g x g^{-1}$.

# Properties

## [[Group Automorphism]]
- $c_g \in Aut(G)$ (It's an automorphism)
