# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Autoregressive Process]] [[Operator]])
> For [[Autoregressive Process]] $X_t$, its autoregressive operator is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \phi(B) = (1 - \phi_1 B - \dots - \phi_p B^p)
> \end{align}
> $$
> With this, we can write the [[Autoregressive Process]] as $\phi(B) X_t = W_t$
