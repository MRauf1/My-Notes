# Definition

[[Light]]-sensitive lining of the [[Eye]].[^1]

Packed with [[Rods]].

Functions in the initial detection of temporal and spatial contrasts and contains specialized [[Neuron]] that subserve [[Color Vision]].

# Parts
- [[Fovea]]
- [[Photoreceptor Cell]]
- [[Ganglion Cell]]
