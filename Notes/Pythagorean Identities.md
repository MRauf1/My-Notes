# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Pythagorean Identities)[^1]
> For any [[Angle]] $\theta$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \cos^2(\theta) + \sin^2(\theta) &= 1 \\
> 1 + \tan^2(\theta) &= \sec^2(\theta) \\
> 1 + \cot^2(\theta) &= \csc^2(\theta)
> \end{align}
> $$

Relates to the [[Pythagorean Theorem]].
