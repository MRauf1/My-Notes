# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Secant [[Function]])[^1]
> Secant function is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sec(\theta) = \frac{1}{\cos(\theta)}
> \end{align}
> $$
> provided $\cos(\theta) \neq 0$.

Has [[Domain]] $\{\theta | \theta \neq \frac{\pi}{2} + \pi k, k \in \mathbb{Z}\}$ and [[Range]] $(-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty)$.
