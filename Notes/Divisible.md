# Definition

If a [[Number|number]] $x$ is a [[Multiple|multiple]] of a number $y$, then $x$ is divisible by $y$.[^1]

> [!example]- Examples 1 (Divisible)
> > [!example]- Example 1.1 (Basic Example)
> > $$
> > \begin{align}
> > 6 \div 3 = 2
> > \end{align}
> > $$
> > $6$ is divisible by $3$.
