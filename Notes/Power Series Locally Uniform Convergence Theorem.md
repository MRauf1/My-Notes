# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Power Series]] [[Sequence of Function Locally Uniform Convergence]] Theorem)
> [[Power Series]] with [[Radius of Convergence]] $R$ and [[Disc of Convergence]] $B(z_0, R)$ [[Absolutely Convergent Infinite Series|converges absolutely]] and [[Sequence of Function Locally Uniform Convergence|locally uniformly]] on the [[Disc of Convergence]] $B(z_0, R)$ and diverges at each [[Point]] $\{z: |z - z_0| > R\}$.
