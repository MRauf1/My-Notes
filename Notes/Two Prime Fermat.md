# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1  (Two Prime Fermat)
> Let $p, q$ be distinct [[Prime Number]], $n = pq$, $m = lcm(p - 1, q - 1)$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> a \in \mathbb{Z}, h \in \mathbb{N}, h \equiv 1\ (\text{mod}\ m) \implies a^h \equiv a\ (\text{mod} n)
> \end{align}
> $$
