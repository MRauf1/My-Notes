# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Logarithm)[^1]
> For a base $b$, the logarithm of $x$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> log_b(x)
> \end{align}
> $$

> [!info] Definition 2 (Natural Logarithm)[^1]
> For a base $e$ ([[Euler's Number]]), the logarithm of $x$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> ln(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
