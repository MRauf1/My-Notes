# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Inverse Function]])[^1]
> For [[Differentiable Function]] $f$ with an [[Inverse Function]] $g$, if $f'(x) \neq 0$ and $y = f(x)$, then $g'(y) = \frac{1}{f'(g(y))}$
