# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Absolute Value|Absolute Value]] [[Division|Quotient]] Rule)[^1]
> For $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> |\frac{a}{b}| = \frac{|a|}{|b|}
> \end{align}
> $$
> given $b \neq 0$
