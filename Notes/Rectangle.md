# Definition

Rectangle is a [[Quadrilateral|quadrilateral]] with $4$ sides and $4$ [[Right Angle|right angles]]. The lengths of the opposite sides are the same, and the rectangle is composed of a length $L$ and width $W$.[^1]

# Properties

## [[Perimeter|Perimeter]]

$$P = 2L + 2W $$

## [[Area|Area]]

$$A = LW$$
