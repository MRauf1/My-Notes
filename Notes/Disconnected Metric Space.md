# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Disconnected [[Metric Space]])[^1]
> Let $E$ be [[Subset]] of [[Metric Space]] $(S, d)$. $E$ is disconnected if one of the following holds true:
> 1) There are [[Open Set]] [[Subset]] $U_1, U_2$ of $S$ such that
> 	1) $(E \cap U_1) \cap (E \cap U_2) = \emptyset$
> 	2) $E = (E \cap U_1) \cup (E \cap U_2)$
> 	3) $E \cap U_1 \neq \emptyset$
> 	4) $E \cap U_2 \neq \emptyset$
> 2) There are [[Disjoint Sets]] non-empty [[Subset]] $A, B$ of $E$ such that $E = A \cup B$ and $A^- \cap B = \emptyset$ and $A \cap B^- = \emptyset$ ([[Closure Set]])

The above two conditions are equivalent.
