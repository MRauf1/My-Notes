# Review[^1]

The author comes up with an analogy of English symbols and Chinese symbols to show that just like how a human can simply manipulate formal Chinese symbols in response to "story" questions, a computer too can be simply manipulating the formal symbols to give answers without understanding even the tiniest bit of the "story". And the author argues that this formal manipulation of symbols is not sufficient enough to create an understanding.

[^1]: [Minds, Brains, and Programs](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/F89IHIZA?page=1)
