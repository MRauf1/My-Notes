# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Frame Field)[^1]
> [[Vector Field]] $E_1, \dots, E_n$ on $\mathbb{R}^n$ constitute a frame field if
> $$
> \begin{align}
> E_i \cdot E_j = \delta_{ij}
> \end{align}
> $$
> for all $i, j$ where $\delta_{ij}$ is the [[Kronecker Delta]].
