# Definition

For a [[Function|function]] $f$, $f$ has a local maximum at the [[Point|point]] $(a, b)$ $\iff$ $\exists$ an open [[Interval Notation|interval]] $I$ such that $f(a) \geq f(x)$ $\forall x \in I$.[^1]
