# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Line Integral Independent of Path]] Theorem)[^1]
> If $V$ is a [[Vector Field]] [[Continuous Function]] on open connected region $D$ and if $\int_C V \cdot d\mathbf{r}$ is [[Line Integral Independent of Path]] in $D$, then $V$ is a [[Conservative Vector Field]].

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=1121)
