# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Chi-Squared Distribution]] [[Addition]])[^1]
> For two [[Chi-Squared Distribution|Chi-Squared Random Variables]] $\chi_1^2(k_1), \chi_2^2(k_2)$, their sum is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \chi_1^2(k_1) + \chi_2^2(k_2) &= \chi^2(k_1 + k_2)
> \end{align}
> $$
