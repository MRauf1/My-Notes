# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Binomial Distribution]] [[Maximum Likelihood Estimation]] [[Expectation]])
> For a MLE $\hat{p} = \frac{x}{n}$ from a $Binomial(n, p)$ distribution, its expectation is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> E[\hat{p}] = p
> \end{align}
> $$

Thus, it is an [[Unbiased Estimator]].
