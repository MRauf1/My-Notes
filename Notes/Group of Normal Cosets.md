# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Group]] of [[Normal Subgroup|Normal]] [[Coset|Cosets]])
> For a [[Group]] $G$ with [[Normal Subgroup]] $N$, $(G/N, \cdot)$ is a group with the [[Operation]] $G/N \times G/N \rightarrow G/N$ defined as
> $$
> \begin{align}
> aN \cdot bN := (ab)N
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $a, b \in G$
