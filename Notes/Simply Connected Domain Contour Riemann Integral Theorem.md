# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Simply Connected Domain]] [[Contour Riemann Integral]] Theorem)
> Let $D$ be [[Simply Connected Domain]] and $f: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be [[Analytic Function]]. If $\gamma$ is [[Closed Curve]] [[Piecewise Smooth Curve]] consisting of finitely many horizontal and vertical segments, then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_{\gamma} f(z) dz = 0
> \end{align}
> $$
