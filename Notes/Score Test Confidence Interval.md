# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Score Test]] [[Confidence Interval]])
> For a [[Maximum Likelihood Estimation]] with [[Hypothesis Test]] $H_0: \beta = \beta_0$, the [[Confidence Interval]] is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \{\beta: |z_S| < z_{\alpha/2}\}
> \end{align}
> $$
> at the confidence level $\alpha$
