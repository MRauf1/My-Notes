# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Separable Equation)[^1]
> When [[First Order Ordinary Differential Equation]] is of the form $\frac{dy}{dx} = g(x) f(y)$ with $h(y) = \frac{1}{f(y)}$ and $h(y) \neq 0$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{dy}{dx} &= g(x) f(y) \\
> &= \frac{g(x)}{h(y)} \\
> h(y) dy &= g(x) dx \\
> \int h(y) dy &= \int g(x) dx
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=631)
