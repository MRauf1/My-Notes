# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Removable [[Isolated Singularity]])
> $z_0 \in \mathbb{C}$ is a removable singularity of [[Function]] $f$ if we can assign a value to $f$ at $z_0$ so that it is [[Complex Analytic Function]] on the [[Open Disk]] $B(z_0, r)$.
> In this case, $f$ is [[Continuous Function]] at $z_0$ and $\lim_{z \rightarrow z_0} f(z)$ exists and $f$ remains [[Bounded Function]] as $z \rightarrow z_0$.
