# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Multinomial Distribution]] [[Covariance]])[^1]
> For a [[Multinomial Distribution]] denoted $Multinomial(n, p_1, \dots, p_c)$, its covariance is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Cov[n_j, n_k] = -n p_j p_k
> \end{align}
> $$
