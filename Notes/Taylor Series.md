# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Taylor [[Infinite Series]])[^1]
> [[Infinite Series]] of the form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!} (x - a)^n
> \end{align}
> $$

This provides an easy way to approximate [[Riemann Integral]] for functions whose [[Antiderivative]] is not possible to find.
