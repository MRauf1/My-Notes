# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Absolute [[Minimum]])[^1]
> Let $c \in D$ where $D$ is the [[Domain]] of [[Function]] $f$. Then $f(c)$ is the absolute minimum of $f$ on $D$ if $f(c) \leq f(x)$ for all $x \in D$.
