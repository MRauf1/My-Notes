# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Infinite [[Limit of Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \lim_{x \rightarrow a} f(x) = \infty
> \end{align}
> $$
> We say that the limit does not exist and diverges to infinity.
> Same goes for $-\infty$.
