# Definition

> [!info] Definition
> [[Vector|Vector]] $\mathbf{v} \in \mathbb{F}^n$ such that
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix}
> v_1 & v_2 & \dots & v_n
> \end{bmatrix}
> \end{align}
> $$

Vector represented as a row.[^1]
