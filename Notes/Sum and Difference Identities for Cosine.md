# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Sum and Difference Identities for [[Cosine Function]])[^1]
> For any [[Angle]] $\alpha, \beta$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \cos(\alpha + \beta) &= \cos(\alpha) \cos(\beta) - \sin(\alpha) \sin(\beta) \\
> \cos(\alpha - \beta) &= \cos(\alpha) \cos(\beta) + \sin(\alpha) \sin(\beta) 
> \end{align}
> $$
