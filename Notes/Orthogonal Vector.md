# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Orthogonal [[Vector]])[^1]
> Two [[Vector]] $\mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w} \in \mathbb{R}^n$ are orthogonal if
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{w} = 0
> \end{align}
> $$
