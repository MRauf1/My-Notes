# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Cross Product]] [[Linearity]])
> For [[Vector]] $\mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w} \mathbb{R}^n$ and $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> a\mathbf{v} \times b\mathbf{w} = (ab)(\mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{w})
> \end{align}
> $$
