# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Power Function]] [[Infinite Series]])[^1]
> [[Infinite Series]] of the form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} c_n x^n
> \end{align}
> $$

> [!info] Definition 2 ([[Power Function]] [[Infinite Series]] Centered at $a$)[^1]
> [[Infinite Series]] of the form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} c_n (x - a)^n
> \end{align}
> $$
> Its coefficients are given by 
> $$
> \begin{align}
> c_n = \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}
> \end{align}
> $$

An infinite [[Polynomial Function]]. They can be added/subtracted/multiplied/divided like polynomials.
