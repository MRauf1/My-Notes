# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Multinomial Distribution]] [[Expectation]]/[[Mean]])[^1]
> For a [[Multinomial Distribution]] denoted $Multinomial(n, p_1, \dots, p_c)$, its expectation/mean is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> E[n_j] = n p_j
> \end{align}
> $$
