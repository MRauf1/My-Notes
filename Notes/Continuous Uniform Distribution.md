# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Continuous]] [[Uniform Distribution]])
> A continuous uniform distribution, with [[Function Support]] $[a, b]$, denoted as $U(a, b)$ has pdf
> $$
> \begin{align}
> f(x) = \begin{cases}\frac{1}{b - a} & x \in [a, b] \\ 0 & x \notin [a, b]\end{cases}
> \end{align}
> $$
