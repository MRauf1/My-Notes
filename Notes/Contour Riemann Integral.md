# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Piecewise Smooth Curve|Contour]] [[Riemann Integral]])
> Let $f: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be [[Continuous Function]] and let $\gamma: [a, b] \rightarrow D$ be a [[Piecewise Smooth Curve]]. The contour integral is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_{\gamma} f(z) dz = \int_a^b f(\gamma(t)) \gamma'(t) dt
> \end{align}
> $$
> where the integrand on the right side of the equation is be piecewise [[Continuous Function]].

This is essentially a [[Complex Number]] version of the [[Line Riemann Integral]] on the complex plane.
