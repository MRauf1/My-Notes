# Definition

Form of [[Consequentialism]] that tries to maximize happiness/pleasure for the greatest number of people.[^1]

When deciding between the happiness of two people, one must be impartial.

# Types
- [[Act Utilitarianism]]
- [[Rule Utilitarianism]]

- [[John Stuart Mill]]
