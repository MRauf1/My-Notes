# Review[^1]

Quite a dense and terse read, but one of the most influential papers in CS. For a modern practitioner, I'd recommend a modern version of the paper. For example, I'd recommend learning this material from [[Jeff Erickson]]'s [[Models of Computation (Erickson)]] textbook.

[^1]: [On Computable Numbers](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/JN7TDAA3?page=1)
