# Definition

- $P(A | B) = 1 - P(A^c | B)$, given $P(B) > 0$
- $P(\emptyset | B) = 0$
- $A_1 \subseteq A_2 \implies P(A_1 | B) \leq P(A_2 | B)$
- $P(A | B) \leq 1$
- $P(A_1 \cup A_2 | B) = P(A_1 | B) + P(A_2 | B) - P(A_1 \cap A_2 | B)$
- $P(A | B) + P(A^c | B) = 1$, but generally, it follows that $P(A | B) + P(A | B^c) \neq 1$
