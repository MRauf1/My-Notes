# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Mesh of [[Partition]])[^1]
> Mesh of [[Partition]] $P$ is the maximum [[Length]] of the subintervals comprising $P$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> mesh(P) = \max\{t_k - t_{k-1} | k = 1, 2, \dots, n\}
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=284)
