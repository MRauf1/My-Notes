# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Causal Autoregressive Process|Causal Representation]] [[Operator]])
> For [[Autoregressive Process]] $X_t$, the causal representation operator is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \psi(B) = (1 + \psi_1 B + \psi_2 B^2 + \dots) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \psi_j B^j
> \end{align}
> $$
