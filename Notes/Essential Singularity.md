# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Essential [[Isolated Singularity]])
> $z_0 \in \mathbb{C}$ is an essential singularity of [[Function]] $f$ if $z_0$ is neither a [[Removable Singularity]] nor [[Pole]].
