# Definition

[[Brain Wave]] of [[Frequency]] of 30-100 Hz. It's the fastest electrical signal in the [[Brain]], linked to peak mental states such as [[Focus]], [[Memory]], [[Learning]].

It usually lasts a split second, but in [[Meditation|meditators]], it has been observed to last more than a minute with extensive practice (>10,000 hours).[^1] They can also activate these waves at will and have 25x greater [[Amplitude]] compared to the control group.

[^1]: https://www.lionsroar.com/how-meditation-changes-your-brain-and-your-life/
