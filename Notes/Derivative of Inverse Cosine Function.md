# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Inverse Cosine Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(\arccos(x)) = - \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}
> \end{align}
> $$
