# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] Power Rule)[^1]
> If $n$ is a [[Real Number]], then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(x^n) = nx^{n-1}
> \end{align}
> $$
