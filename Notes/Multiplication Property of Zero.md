# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Multiplication Property of Zero)
> $\forall x$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> x \cdot 0 = 0
> \end{align}
> $$

The [[Multiplication|product]] of any number and $0$ is $0$. [^1]

> > [!example]- Example 1.1 (Multiplication Property of Zero)
> > $$

> > 8 \cdot 0 = 0
> > \end{align}
> > $$
