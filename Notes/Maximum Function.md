# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Maximum]] [[Function]])[^1]
> For [[Function]] $f, g$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \max(f, g) = \frac{1}{2} (f + g) + \frac{1}{2} |f - g|
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=141)
