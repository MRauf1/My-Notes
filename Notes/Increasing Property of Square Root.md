# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Increasing Property of [[Square Root|Square Root]])[^1]
> For $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> 0 \leq a \leq b \implies \sqrt{a} \leq \sqrt{b}
> \end{align}
> $$

Square root on positive numbers preserves the [[Inequality|inequality]] sign.
