# Review[^1]

A very extensive book on the world's most popular and largest religions although while discussing them, it also delves into some of the more ancient religions that have preceded these. Very detailed and dense, it is a very long read, but explores the religions in great detail. It is a very good introduction to the study of religion and the world's religions. Beside the most popular ones, the last section dives into the recently emerging new "religions" too.

[^1]: [Invitation to World Religions](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/LWQR9RH4?page=1)
