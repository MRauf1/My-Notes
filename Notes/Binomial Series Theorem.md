# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Binomial]] [[Infinite Series]] Theorem)[^1]
> If $\alpha \in \mathbb{R}$ and $|x| < 1$, then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> (1 + x)^{\alpha} = 1 + \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{\alpha!}{(\alpha - k)!k!} x^k
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=264)
