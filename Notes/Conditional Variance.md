# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Conditional Probability|Conditional]] [[Variance]])
> For [[Random Variable]] $X, Y$, the conditional variance of $X$ given $Y = y$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[X | Y = y] = E[(X - \mu_{X | y})^2 | Y = y]
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $\mu_{X | y} = E[X | Y = y]$
