# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Isaac Newton]] Second Law)[^1]
> For [[Force]] $\mathbf{F}$, [[Mass]] $m$, [[Acceleration]] $\mathbf{a}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sum \mathbf{F} = m \mathbf{a}
> \end{align}
> $$
