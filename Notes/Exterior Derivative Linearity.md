# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Exterior Derivative]] [[Linearity]])[^1]
> For [[Differential Form]] $\phi, \psi$ and $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> d(a \phi + b \psi) = a \cdot d\phi + b \cdot d \psi
> \end{align}
> $$
