# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Curve]] [[Arc Length]])[^1]
> Given a [[Smooth Curve]] $\alpha$, the arc length from $t = a$ to $t = b$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_a^b ||\alpha'(t)|| dt
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Differential Geometry](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/F6CCEWIU?page=68)
