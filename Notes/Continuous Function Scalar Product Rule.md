# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Continuous Function]] Scalar Product Rule)[^1]
> If [[Function]] $f$ is [[Continuous Function]] at $a$ and $c \in \mathbb{R}$, then $cf$ is also continuous at $a$.
