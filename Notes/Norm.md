# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Norm)[^1]
> Norm of a [[Vector]]/[[Point]] $\mathbf{p} \in \mathbb{R}^n$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> ||\mathbf{p}|| = \sqrt{\mathbf{p} \cdot \mathbf{p}}
> \end{align}
> $$
