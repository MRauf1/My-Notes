# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Secant Function]])[^1]
> If $f(x) = \sec(x)$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(\sec(x)) = \sec(x) \tan(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
