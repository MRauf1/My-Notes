# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Quantifier]] [[Distributive Property]])
> [[Universal Quantifier]] distributes over [[Conjunction Argument]]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \forall x (P(x) \land Q(x)) \iff \forall x P(x) \land \forall x Q(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
> [[Existential Quantifier]] distributes over [[Disjunction Argument]]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \exists x (P(x) \lor Q(x)) \iff \exists P(x) \lor \exists Q(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
