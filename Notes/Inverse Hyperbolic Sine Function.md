# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Inverse Function]] [[Hyperbolic Sine Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sinh^{-1}(x) = \ln(x + \sqrt{x^2 + 1})
> \end{align}
> $$
> [[Domain]] is $\mathbb{R}$ and [[Range]] is $\mathbb{R}$.
