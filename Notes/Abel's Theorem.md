# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Abel's Theorem)[^1]
> Let $f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} c_n (x - a)^n$ be a [[Power Series]] with finite positive [[Radius of Convergence]].
> 1) If the series converges at $x = a - R$, then $f$ is [[Continuous Function]] at $x = a - R$
> 2) If the series converges at $x = a + R$, then $f$ is [[Continuous Function]] at $x = a + R$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=222)
