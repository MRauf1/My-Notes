# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Branch)
> Let $D \subseteq \mathbb{C}$, $f: D \rightarrow 2^{\mathbb{C}}$ a [[Multi-Valued Function]]. Let $F = \{f(z) | z \in D\} \subseteq \mathbb{C}$ be the [[Image]] of $f$ on $D$. We say that $g$ is a branch of $f$ on $D$ if
> 1) $\forall z \in D, g(z) \in F$
> 2) $g$ is [[Continuous Function]] on $D$
