# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Horizontal Line)[^1]
> [[Graph|Graph]] of an [[Equation|equation]] in the form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> y = b
> \end{align}
> $$

It passes through the $y$-[[Axis|axis]] at the [[Point|point]] $(0, b)$.

The [[Slope|slope]] is $0$.[^2]
