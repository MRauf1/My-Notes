# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Line Riemann Integral]] Independent of Path)
> For 2 paths/[[Curve]] $C_1, C_2$ with the same endpoints on a [[Vector Field]] $V$, the line integral of $V$ is independent of path if
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_{C_1} V \cdot d\mathbf{r} = \int_{C_2} V \cdot d\mathbf{r}
> \end{align}
> $$
