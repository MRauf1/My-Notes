# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Slant [[Asymptote]])[^1]
> [[Line]] $y = mx + b$ with $m \neq 0$ is a slant asymptote for a [[Function]] $y = f(x)$ if as $x \rightarrow -\infty$ or $x \rightarrow \infty$, $f(x) \rightarrow mx + b$
