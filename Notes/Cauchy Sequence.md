# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Cauchy [[Sequence]])[^1]
> Sequence $(s_n)$ is a Cauchy sequence if for all $\epsilon > 0$, there exists a number $N$ such that
> $$
> \begin{align}
> m, n > N \implies |s_n - s_m| < \epsilon
> \end{align}
> $$
