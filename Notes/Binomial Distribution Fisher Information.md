# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Binomial Distribution]] [[Maximum Likelihood Estimation]] [[Fisher Information]])
> For a MLE $\hat{p} = \frac{x}{n}$ from a $Binomial(n, p)$ distribution, its Fisher information is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \iota(p) = \frac{n}{p (1 - p)}
> \end{align}
> $$
