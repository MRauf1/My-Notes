# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Vertical [[Asymptote]])[^1]
> [[Line]] $x = c$ is a vertical asymptote for a [[Function]] $y = f(x)$ if as $x \rightarrow c^-$ or $x \rightarrow c^+$, either $f(x) \rightarrow \infty$ or $f(x) \rightarrow -\infty$
