# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Vector [[Projection]])[^1]
> Vector projection of [[Vector]] $\mathbf{b}$ onto [[Vector]] $\mathbf{a}$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> proj_{\mathbf{a}}\mathbf{b} = (\frac{\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b}}{||\mathbf{a}||}) \frac{\mathbf{a}}{||\mathbf{a}||} = \frac{\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b}}{||\mathbf{a}||^2} \mathbf{a}
> \end{align}
> $$
