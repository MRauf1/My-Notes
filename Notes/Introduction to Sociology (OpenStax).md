# Review[^1]

Book by [[OpenStax]] on [[Sociology]].

I found it to be pretty meh. There were more than a handful of typos throughout the book and most of the material is not that revolutionary. The book spends more time discussing the present day statistics of various social issues rather than going in-depth into the field of sociology itself. That's especially problematic considered that these statistics go out-of-date very quickly.

[^1]: [Introduction to Sociology](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DU6GPJWT?page=1)
