# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Poisson Distribution]] [[Variance]])[^1]
> For a [[Poisson Distribution]] denoted $Poisson(\lambda)$, its variance is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[X] = \lambda
> \end{align}
> $$
