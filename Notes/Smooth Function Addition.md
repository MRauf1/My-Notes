# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Smooth Function]] [[Addition]])[^1]
> For smooth functions $f, g$, their sum is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> (f + g)(\mathbf{p}) := f(\mathbf{p}) + g(\mathbf{p})
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Differential Geometry](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/F6CCEWIU?page=20)
