# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Nth Root]] Quotient Rule)[^1]
> For [[Real Number]] $x, y, y \neq 0$ and [[Natural Number]] $n$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sqrt[n]{\frac{x}{y}} &= \frac{\sqrt[n]{x}}{\sqrt[n]{y}}
> \end{align}
> $$
