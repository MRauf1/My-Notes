# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Differentiable Function]] Theorem)[^1]
> If all the [[Partial Derivative]] of $f$ exist near $\mathbf{a}$ and are [[Continuous Function]] at $\mathbf{a}$, then $f$ is [[Differentiable Function]] at $\mathbf{a}$

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=963)
