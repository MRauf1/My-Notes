# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Epsilon Neighborhood)
> In a [[Metric Space]] $(S, d)$, the epsilon neighborhood of a [[Point]] $x \in S$ with [[Radius]] $\epsilon > 0$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> B(x, r) = \{a \in S | d(x, a) < \epsilon\}
> \end{align}
> $$

It is an [[Open n-Ball]] of [[Radius]] $\epsilon$.
