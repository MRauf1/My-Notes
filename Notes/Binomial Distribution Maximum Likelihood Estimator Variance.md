# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Binomial Distribution]] [[Maximum Likelihood Estimation]] [[Variance]])
> For a MLE $\hat{p} = \frac{x}{n}$ from a $Binomial(n, p)$ distribution, its variance is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[\hat{p}] = \frac{p(1 - p)}{n}
> \end{align}
> $$
