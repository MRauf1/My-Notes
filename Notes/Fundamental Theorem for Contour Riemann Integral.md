# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Fundamental Theorem of Calculus]] for [[Contour Riemann Integral]])
> Let $D, f, F$ be as in [[Primitive]] note and $\gamma: [a, b] \rightarrow D$ be a [[Piecewise Smooth Curve]]. Then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_{\gamma} f(z) dz = F(\gamma(b)) - F(\gamma(a))
> \end{align}
> $$

> [!abstract] Corollary 2 ([[Closed Curve]])
> For Theorem 1, if in addition, $\gamma$ is [[Closed Curve]], then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_{\gamma} f(z) dz = F(\gamma(b)) - F(\gamma(a)) = 0
> \end{align}
> $$
