# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Exterior [[Derivative]])[^1]
> If $\phi = \sum_{i} f_i dx_i$ is a [[1-Form]] on $\mathbb{R}^n$, then the exterior derivative of $\phi$ is a $2$-form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> d\phi = \sum_{i} df_i \land dx_i
> \end{align}
> $$
