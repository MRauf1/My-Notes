# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1
> Let $s_n$ be a [[Convergent Sequence]] of real numbers such that $s_n \neq 0$ for all $n \in \mathbb{N}$ and $lim(s_n) = s \neq 0$. Then $inf(\{|s_n| : n \in \mathbb{N}\}) > 0$, where $inf$ is the [[Infimum]].
