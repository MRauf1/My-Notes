# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Sum of the Measures of the Angles of a Triangle)[^1]
> For any $\Delta ABC$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> m \angle A + m \angle B + m \angle C = 360
> \end{align}
> $$
