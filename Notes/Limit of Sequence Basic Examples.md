# Definition

- $lim((1 + \frac{1}{n})^n) = e$[^1]
- $lim(\frac{1}{n^p}) = 0$ for $p > 0$[^2]
- $lim(a^n) = 0$ if $|a| < 1$
- $lim(n^{\frac{1}{n}}) = 1$
- $lim(a^{\frac{1}{n}}) = 1$ for $a > 0$
