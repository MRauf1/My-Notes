# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Limit of Function]] Basic Properties)[^1]
> Assume that $\lim_{x \rightarrow a} f(x)$ exists and $n \in \mathbb{N}$. Then
> - $\lim_{x \rightarrow a} (f(x))^n = (\lim_{x \rightarrow a} f(x))^n$
