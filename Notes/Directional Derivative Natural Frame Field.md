# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Directional Derivative]] [[Natural Frame Field]])[^1]
> Applying the directional derivative on the natural frame field $U_i$, we get the [[Function]]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> U_i[f](\mathbf{p}) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_i}(\mathbf{p})
> \end{align}
> $$
