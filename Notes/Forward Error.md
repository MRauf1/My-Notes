# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Forward [[Error]])[^1]
> For a [[Function]] $y = f(x)$, the forward error is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \Delta y = \hat{y} - y
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $\hat{y} = \hat{f}(x) = f(\hat{x})$

[^1]: [Scientific Computing](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/UQ4SGXEK?page=23)
