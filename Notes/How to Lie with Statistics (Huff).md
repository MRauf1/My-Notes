# Review[^1]

A book about the various ways that statistics can be misconstrued to provide "false" information or misinform the reader. A lot of the knowledge is basic statistics, and as such, simply a review for me, but for those not very knowledgeable about the field, it is a good introduction to the types of misinformation one should keep their eyes peeled for. The book is a short and easy read, so it's not hard to get through either.

[^1]: [How to Lie with Statistics](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/QATUIXFK?page=1)
