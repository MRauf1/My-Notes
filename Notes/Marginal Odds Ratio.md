# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Marginal Probability|Marginal]] [[Odds Ratio]])
> For a [[Three-Way Contingency Table]], the marginal odds ratio is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \theta_{XY} = \frac{\mu_{11+} \mu_{22+}}{\mu_{12+} \mu_{21+}}
> \end{align}
> $$
> estimated by
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \hat{\theta}_{XY(k)} = \frac{n_{11+} n_{22+}}{n_{12+} n_{21+}}
> \end{align}
> $$
> If $\theta_{XY} = 1$, then there is no marginal association.
