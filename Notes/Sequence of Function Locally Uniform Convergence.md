# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Sequence of Function]] Locally [[Sequence of Function Uniform Convergence|Uniform Convergence]])
> [[Sequence of Function]] $(f_n) \rightarrow f$ is locally uniform on $D$ if for all $z \in D$, there exists $r > 0$ such that $(f_n) \rightarrow f$ [[Sequence of Function Uniform Convergence|uniformly]] on [[Epsilon Neighborhood]] $B(z, r)$.
