# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Absolute Value|Absolute Value]] [[Exponentiation|Power]] Rule)[^1]
> For $a \in \mathbb{R}$ and $n \in \mathbb{Z}$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> |a^n| = |a|^n
> \end{align}
> $$
> whenever $a^n$ is defined
