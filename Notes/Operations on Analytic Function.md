# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Operation]] on [[Analytic Function]])
> Given [[Analytic Function]] $f, g$,
> 1) $f + g$ is [[Analytic Function]]
> 2) $f - g$ is [[Analytic Function]]
> 3) $f \cdot g$ is [[Analytic Function]]
> 4) $\frac{f}{g}$ is [[Analytic Function]] for $g \neq 0$
> 5) $f \circ g$ is [[Analytic Function]]
