# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Taylor Series]] Equal to [[Function]] Theorem)[^1]
> If $f(x) = T_n(x) + R_n(x)$ where $T_n(x)$ is the $n$th degree [[Taylor Polynomial]] and $R_n(x) = f(x) - T_n(x)$ and $\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} R_n(x) = 0$ on the interval $I$, then $f$ is equal to its [[Taylor Series]] on the interval $I$.

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=793)
