# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Absolute Condition Number)[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \text{Absolute Condition Number} &= \frac{|\Delta y|}{|\Delta x|} \\
> &= \frac{|\text{Absolute Forward Error}|}{|\text{Absolute Backward Error}|}
> \end{align}
> $$
