# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Supremum]] [[Addition]] Rule)[^1]
> For non-empty [[Bounded Set|bounded subsets]] $A, B$ of $\mathbb{R}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> sup(A + B) = sup(A) + sup(B)
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=41)
