# Standard of Time

The second is the duration of $9,192,631,770$ [[Vibration|vibrations]] of a (specified) [[Radiation|radiation]] emitted by a (specified) [[Isotope|isotope]] of the [[Cesium|cesium]] [[Atom|atom]].[^1]
