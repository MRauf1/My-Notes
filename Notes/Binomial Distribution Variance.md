# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Binomial Distribution]] [[Variance]])[^1]
> For a [[Binomial Distribution]] denoted $Binomial(n, p)$, its [[Variance]] is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[X] = np(1 - p)
> \end{align}
> $$
