# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Jordan Curve]] Theorem)
> A [[Simple Curve]] [[Closed Curve]] in $\mathbb{C}$ divides $\mathbb{C}$ into two [[Disjoint Sets]] [[Connected Metric Space|connected]] [[Open Set]]:
> 1) One [[Bounded Set]] that is inside the [[Curve]].
> 2) One [[Unbounded Set]] that is outside the [[Curve]].
