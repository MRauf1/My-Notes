# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Riemann Integral]] [[Triangle Inequality]])[^1]
> If $f$ is integrable on $[a, b]$, then $|f|$ is integrable on $[a, b]$ and
> $$
> \begin{align}
> |\int_a^b f| \leq \int_a^b |f|
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=293)
