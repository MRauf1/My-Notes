# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Inverse Property of Addition)[^1]
> $\forall x$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> x + (-x) = 0
> \end{align}
> $$

[[Addition|Sum]] of a number and its [[Opposite Number|negative]] (additive inverse) is $0$ (additive identity).
