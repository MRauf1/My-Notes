# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Logarithm]] Change of Base)[^1]
> Let $a, b > 0, a, b \neq 1$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> log_a(x) = \frac{log_b(x)}{log_b(a)}\ \text{for all $x \in \mathbb{R}^+$}
> \end{align}
> $$
