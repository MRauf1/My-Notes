# Definition

> [!info] Definition (Inverse [[Secant Function]])[^1]
> [[Inverse Function]] of [[Secant Function]].
> Has [[Domain]] $(-\infty, -1] \cup  [1, \infty)$ and [[Range]] $[0, \frac{\pi}{2}) \cup (\frac{\pi}{2}, \pi]$

According to the textbook, the ranges may be different. Look into [^1][^2].
