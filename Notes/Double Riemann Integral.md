# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Double [[Riemann Integral]])[^1]
> Double integral of $f$ over region $R$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_a^b \int_c^d f(x, y) dx dy = \int \int_R f(x, y) dA = \lim_{m, n \rightarrow \infty} \sum_{i=1}^m \sum_{j=1}^n f(x_{ij}^*, y_{ij}^*) \Delta A
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $\Delta A = \frac{b - a}{m} \frac{d - c}{n}$
> if the [[Limit of Function]] exists.
