# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Inverse Hyperbolic Tangent Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}\tanh^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{1 - x^2}
> \end{align}
> $$
