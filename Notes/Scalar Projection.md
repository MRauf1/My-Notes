# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Scalar [[Projection]])[^1]
> Scalar projection of [[Vector]] $\mathbf{b}$ onto [[Vector]] $\mathbf{a}$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> comp_{\mathbf{a}}\mathbf{b} = \frac{\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b}}{||\mathbf{a}||}
> \end{align}
> $$

It is the signed [[Norm]] of the [[Vector Projection]] of $\mathbf{b}$ onto $\mathbf{a}$.

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=843)
