# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Cross [[Correlation]])
> For two [[Stochastic Process]] $X_t, Y_t$, their cross correlation is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \rho_{xy}(h) = \frac{\gamma_{xy}(h)}{\sqrt{\gamma_x(0) \gamma_y(0)}} = \frac{Cov[X_{t + h}, Y_t]}{\sqrt{Var[X_t] Var[Y_t]}}
> \end{align}
> $$
