# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Nth Root]] Product Rule)[^1]
> For [[Real Number]] $x, y$ and [[Natural Number]] $n$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sqrt[n]{xy} &= \sqrt[n]{x} \cdot \sqrt[n]{y}
> \end{align}
> $$
