# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Position [[Vector]] in 3D)[^1]
> A [[Function]] $s: \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R^3}$ describing the velocity of a particle
> $$
> \begin{align}
> v(t) = \frac{d \mathbf{s}}{dt}
> \end{align}
> $$
