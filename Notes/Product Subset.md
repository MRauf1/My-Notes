# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Product [[Subset]])
> For a [[Group]] $G$ with subsets $A, B \subseteq G$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> AB := \{ab | a \in A, b \in B\} \subseteq G \\
> BA := \{ba | a \in A, b \in B\} \subseteq G \\
> \end{align}
> $$

# Properties

## [[Subgroup]]
- If $A, B \leq G$, then $AB$ is a subgroup $\iff$ $AB = BA$
