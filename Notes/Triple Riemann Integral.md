# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Triple [[Riemann Integral]])[^1]
> Triple integral of $f$ over region $R$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_a^b \int_c^d \int_e^f f(x, y, z) dx dy dz = \int \int_R f(x, y, z) dV = \lim_{m, n, p \rightarrow \infty} \sum_{i=1}^m \sum_{j=1}^n \sum_{k=1}^p f(x_{ijk}^*, y_{ijk}^*, z_{ijk}^*) \Delta V
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $\Delta V = \frac{b - a}{m} \frac{d - c}{n} \frac{f - e}{p}$
> if the [[Limit of Function]] exists.
