# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Chi-Squared [[Probability Distribution]])[^1]
> Denoted as $\chi^2(k)$, where $k$ is the [[Degree of Freedom]].
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P(x) &= ... \\
> E[X] &= k \\
> Var[X] &= 2k
> \end{align}
> $$
