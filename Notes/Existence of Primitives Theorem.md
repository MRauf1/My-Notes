# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Existence of [[Primitive]] Theorem)
> Let $D$ be [[Simply Connected Domain]] and $f: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ [[Analytic Function]], then $f$ has a [[Primitive]] $F: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$.

> [!abstract] Corollary 2 (Corollary)
> Let $D$ be [[Simply Connected Domain]] and $f: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ [[Analytic Function]]. Then, for any [[Closed Curve]] [[Piecewise Smooth Curve]] $\gamma$ in $D$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \int_{\gamma} f(z) dz = 0
> \end{align}
> $$
