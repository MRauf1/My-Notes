# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Standard Deviation)
> For a [[Random Variable]] $X$, its standard deviation is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> SD(X) = \sqrt{Var[X]}
> \end{align}
> $$

Standard deviation expresses variability on the same scale as the [[Random Variable]].[^1]
