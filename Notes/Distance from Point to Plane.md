# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Distance]] from [[Point]] to [[Plane]])[^1]
> Given a [[Point]] $\mathbf{p}_1$ and any [[Point]] on the [[Plane]] $\mathbf{p}_0$ with $\mathbf{b} = \mathbf{p}_1 - \mathbf{p}_0$, then the distance $D$ from $\mathbf{p}_1$ to the plane is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> D = |comp_{\mathbf{n}}\mathbf{b}|
> \end{align}
> $$
