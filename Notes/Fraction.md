# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Fraction)
> $x_1, x_2 \in \mathbb{Z}, x_2 \neq 0$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{x_1}{x_2}
> \end{align}
> $$

Fraction is a number that represents a part of a whole (also represents [[Division|division]]). $x_1$ is the numerator, and $x_2$ is the denominator.[^1]
