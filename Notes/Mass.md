# Definition

Measure of matter within an object.

Within [[Mechanics]], can be viewed as a quantitative measure of the resistance of a body to [[Acceleration]] by a given [[Force]].

# Standard of Mass

The kilogram is the mass of a [[Platinum|platinum]]-[[Iridium|iridium]] [[Cylinder|cylinder]] kept at the International Bureau of Weights and Measures.[^1]
