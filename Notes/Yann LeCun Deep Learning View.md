# View

Yann LeCun argues that training effective AI models consists of 3 parts:
1) Self-Supervised Pre-Training (makes up the core of the training process) (says it's the filling of the cake)
2) Supervised Fine-Tuning (says it's the icing of the cake)
3) Reinforcement Learning (says it's the cherry on the top of the cake)
