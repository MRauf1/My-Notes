# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Simply Connected [[Domain (Topology)]])
> [[Domain (Topology)]] $D$ is simply connected if whenever $\gamma$ is a [[Simple Curve]] [[Closed Curve]] in $D$, the inside of $\gamma$ is also in $D$.
> Namely, no [[Closed Curve]] can circulate around [[Point]] outside of $D$. $D$ has no "holes".
> Equivalently, [[Domain (Topology)]] $D \subseteq \mathbb{C}$ is simply connected if and only if $\hat{\mathbb{C}} \setminus D$ is a [[Connected Metric Space|connected set]] in $\hat{\mathbb{C}}$, [[Extended Complex Plane]] ($\hat{\mathbb{C}} = \mathbb{C} \cup \infty$).
