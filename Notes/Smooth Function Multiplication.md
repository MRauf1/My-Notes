# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Smooth Function]] [[Multiplication]])[^1]
> For smooth functions $f, g$, their product is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> (f \cdot g)(\mathbf{p}) := f(\mathbf{p}) \cdot g(\mathbf{p})
> \end{align}
> $$
