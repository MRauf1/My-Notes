# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Taylor's Inequality)[^1]
> If $|f^{(n + 1)}(x)| \leq M$ for $|x - a| \leq d$, then the remainder $R_n(x)$ of the [[Taylor Series]] satisfies
> $$
> \begin{align}
> |R_n(x)| \leq \frac{M}{(n + 1)!} |x - a|^{n + 1}
> \end{align}
> $$
> for $|x - a| \leq d$

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=794)
