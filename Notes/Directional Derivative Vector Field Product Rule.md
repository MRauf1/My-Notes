# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Directional Derivative]] [[Vector Field]] [[Derivative Product Rule]])[^1]
>Let $f, g$ be [[Smooth Function]] on $\mathbb{R}^3$ and $V$ [[Vector Field]]. Then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> V[fg] = V[f] g + f V[g]
> \end{align}
> $$
