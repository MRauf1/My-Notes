# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Kernel)
> For a [[Group Homomorphism]] $\phi: G \rightarrow H$, its kernel is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> ker(\phi) := \{g \in G | \phi(g) = e_H\} \leq G
> \end{align}
> $$

# Properties

## [[Normal Subgroup]]
- $ker(\phi)$ is a normal subgroup
