# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Multinomial Distribution]] [[Variance]])[^1]
> For a [[Multinomial Distribution]] denoted $Multinomial(n, p_1, \dots, p_c)$, its variance is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[n_j] = n p_j(1 - p_j)
> \end{align}
> $$
