# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Inverse Cosecant Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(arccsc(x)) = - \frac{1}{x \sqrt{x^2 - 1}}
> \end{align}
> $$
