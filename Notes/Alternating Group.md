# Definition

[[Group]] of [[Permutation Parity|Even]] [[Permutation|permutations]] of a finite [[Set]]. It is denoted as $A_n$, where $n$ is the number of elements in the set.

It is a [[Subgroup]] of $S_n$.

# Properties

## [[Group Order]]
- $A_n = \frac{n!}{2}$
