# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Stationarity]] [[Stochastic Process]])
> Finite [[Variance]] [[Stochastic Process]] where
> 1) $E[X_t]$ is constant (does not depend on time $t$)
> 2) $Cov[t, s]$ depends on times $t, s$ only through their time difference
