# Review[^1]

Unfortunately, I didn't find this book enlightening or all that useful to me as a lot of the concepts that the book explores are already things that I either already know or already do. Don't get me wrong, it has good ideas, such as being mindful about your technology uses and such, but I'm not the target audience.
