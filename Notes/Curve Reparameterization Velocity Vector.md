# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Curve Reparameterization]] [[Velocity Vector of Smooth Curve]])[^1]
> If $\beta$ is the reparameterization of $\alpha$ by $h$, then
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \beta'(s) = \frac{dh}{ds}(s) \cdot \alpha'(h(s))
> \end{align}
> $$
