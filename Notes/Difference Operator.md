# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Difference [[Operator]])
> For a [[Stochastic Process]] $X_t$, the difference operator applied on it is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \nabla X_t &= X_t - X_{t-1} \\
> \nabla^d X_t &= (1 - B)^d X_t
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $B$ is the [[Backshift Operator]].
