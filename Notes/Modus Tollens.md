# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Modus Ponens)
> For [[Proposition|statements]] $P, Q$, it is the [[Argument|argument]] of the form[^1]
> - $P \implies Q$
> - $\neg Q$
> - Therefore, $\neg P$

[^1]: [Introduction to Philosophy](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/M84L5RRJ?page=161)
