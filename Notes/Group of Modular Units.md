# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Group of Modular Units of $Z_n$)
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \Phi(n) = \{x \in Z_n | x\ \text{has a multiplicative inverse}\} \subseteq Z_n
> \end{align}
> $$

$(\Phi(n), \cdot)$ is an [[Abelian Group]]
