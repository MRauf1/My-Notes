# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Hyperbolic Function]] [[Tangent Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \tanh(x) = \frac{\sinh(x)}{\cosh(x)}
> \end{align}
> $$
> [[Domain]] is $\mathbb{R}$ and [[Range]] is $(-1, 1)$.

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=291)
