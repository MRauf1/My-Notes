# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Pierre Fermat]] Theorem)[^1]
> If $f$ has [[Local Minimum]] or [[Local Maximum]] at $\mathbf{c}$ and $\nabla f(\mathbf{c})$ exists (all [[Partial Derivative]] exist), then $\nabla f(\mathbf{c}) = 0$.
> Alternatively, if the above antecedent is true, then $\mathbf{c}$ is a [[Critical Number]].

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=311)
