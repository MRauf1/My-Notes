# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Auto [[Correlation]])
> For [[Stochastic Process]] $X_t$, its auto correlation is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \rho(h) = \frac{\gamma(h)}{\gamma(0)}
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $\gamma$ is its [[Auto Covariance]].
