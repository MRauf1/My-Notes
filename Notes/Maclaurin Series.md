# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Maclaurin [[Infinite Series]])[^1]
> [[Infinite Series]] of the form
> $$
> \begin{align}
> f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(0)}{n!} x^n
> \end{align}
> $$

[[Taylor Series]] centered at $0$.
