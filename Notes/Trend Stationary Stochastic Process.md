# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Trend]] [[Stationarity]] [[Stochastic Process]])
> [[Time Series]] that have [[Stationarity]] around a [[Trend]].
> When $Y_t$ is [[Stationary Stochastic Process]] and $f(t)$ is [[Deterministic]] [[Function]], then $X_t = f(t) + Y_t$ is called trend stationary stochastic process/time series.
