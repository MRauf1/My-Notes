# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Polynomial)[^1]
> For $x_1, a_1, b_1, ..., x_n, a_n, b_n$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> a_1 x_1^{b_1} + ... + a_n x_n^{b_n}
> \end{align}
> $$

Polynomial is $\geq 1$ [[Monomial|monomials]] combined with [[Addition|addition]].
