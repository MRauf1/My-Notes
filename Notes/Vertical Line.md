# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Vertical Line)[^1]
> [[Graph|Graph]] of an [[Equation|equation]] in the form 
> $$
> \begin{align}
> x = a
> \end{align}
> $$

It passes through the $x$-[[Axis|axis]] at [[Point|point]] $(a, 0)$.

The [[Slope|slope]] is undefined.[^2]
