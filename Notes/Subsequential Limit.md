# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Subsequential Limit)[^1]
> Let $(s_n)$ be a [[Sequence]] in $\mathbb{R}$. The subsequential limit is a real number, $+\infty$, or $-\infty$ that is the [[Limit of Sequence]] of some [[Subsequence]] of $(s_n)$.
