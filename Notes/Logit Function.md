# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Logit [[Function]])
> For a probability $p$, the logit function of it is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> logit(p) = ln(\frac{p}{1 - p})
> \end{align}
> $$
> It is also known as log [[Odds]].

The [[Logarithm]] of the [[Odds]].
