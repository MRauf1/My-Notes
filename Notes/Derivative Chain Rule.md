# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] Chain Rule)[^1]
> If $f, g$ are both [[Differentiable Function]],
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(f(g(x))) &= f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x) \\
> \frac{dy}{dx} &= \frac{dy}{du} \frac{du}{dx}
> \end{align}
> $$
