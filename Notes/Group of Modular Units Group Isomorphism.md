# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Group of Modular Units]] [[Group Isomorphism]])
> If $a, b \geq 1$, $a, b$ are [[Relatively Prime]], $m = ab$, then there is an isomorphism $\phi: \Phi(m) \rightarrow \Phi(a) \times \Phi(b)$ with $\phi([x]_m) = ([x]_a, [x]_b)$
