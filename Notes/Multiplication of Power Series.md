# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Multiplication]] of [[Power Series]])
> For two [[Power Series]] $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_k (z - z_0)^k$, $\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} b_j (z - z_0)^j$ with [[Radius of Convergence]] $\geq R > 0$ for each, then the product series also has [[Radius of Convergence]] $\geq R > 0$ and
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} a_k (z - z_0)^k \cdot \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} b_j (z - z_0)^j = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} c_n (z - z_0)^n
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $c_n \sum_{k=0}^n a_k b_{n - k}$.
