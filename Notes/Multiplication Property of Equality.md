# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Multiplication Property of Equality)
> For any $a, b, c$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> a = b \iff ac = bc
> \end{align}
> $$

[[Multiplication|Multiplying]] both sides of the [[Equation|equation]] by the same value does not change the validity of the equation.[^1]
