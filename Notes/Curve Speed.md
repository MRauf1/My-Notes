# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Curve]] [[Speed]])[^1]
> Given a [[Smooth Curve]] $\alpha: I \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^3$ with [[Velocity Vector]] $\alpha'(t)$, the speed of $\alpha$ at $t$ is a [[Function]] $v: I \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ with
> $$
> \begin{align}
> v(t) = ||\alpha'(t)||
> \end{align}
> $$
