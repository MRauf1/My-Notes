# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Difference of Proportions)
> For measuring [[Homogeneity]], the difference of proportions is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> p_1 - p_2
> \end{align}
> $$
> If $p_1 - p_2 = 0$, then we have homogeneity.
