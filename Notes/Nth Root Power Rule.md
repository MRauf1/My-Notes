# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Nth Root]] Power Rule)[^1]
> For [[Real Number]] $x$ and [[Natural Number]] $m, n$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> (\sqrt[n]{x})^m &= \sqrt[n]{x^m}
> \end{align}
> $$
