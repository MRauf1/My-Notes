# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Zero Exponent Property)[^1]
> For $x \neq 0$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> x^0 = 1
> \end{align}
> $$

[[Number|Number]] raised to $0$th [[Exponentiation|power]] is $1$.

> [!example]- Examples 1 (Zero Exponent Property)
> > [!example]- Example 1.1 (Constant with a Zero Exponent)
> > $$3^0 = 1$$
