# Definition

$r \sin(\theta)$ is the y-coordinate of a [[Point]] on a [[Circle]] centered at [[Origin]] with [[Radius]] $r$ that has been rotated by $\theta$ degrees counter-clockwise from the x-axis.[^1]

For a [[Right Triangle]] as depicted below, $\sin(\theta) = \frac{b}{c}$. Memorized as [[SOH-CAH-TOA]].

![[Pasted image 20260413151135.png]]

Has [[Domain]] $(-\infty, \infty)$ and range $[-1, 1]$.

> [!info] Definition 2 (Sine Function as [[Complex Number]])
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \sin(z) = \frac{e^{iz} - e^{-iz}}{2i}
> \end{align}
> $$
