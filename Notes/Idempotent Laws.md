# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Idempotent Laws)
> For [[Proposition|statement]] $P$[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P \land P = P \\
> P \lor P = P
> \end{align}
> $$


[^1]: [HOW TO PROVE IT: A Structured Approach, Second Edition](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/THI2Q4PN?page=35)
