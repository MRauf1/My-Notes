# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Helix in $\mathbb{R}^3$)[^1]
> A helix with a radius $a > 0$, and [[Slope]] $b \neq 0$ in the $xy$-plane of $\mathbb{R}^3$ is a [[Smooth Curve]] that is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \alpha(t) = (acos(t), asin(t), bt)
> \end{align}
> $$
