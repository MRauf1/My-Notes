# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Improper Fraction) [^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \mathrm{Fraction}\ \frac{x_1}{x_2}, \quad \mathrm{where}\ x_1 \geq x_2
> \end{align}
> $$

[[Fraction|Fraction]] whose numerator is greater than or equal to the denominator.
