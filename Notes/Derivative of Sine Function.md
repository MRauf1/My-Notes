# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Sine Function]])[^1]
> If $f(x) = \sin(x)$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(\sin(x)) = \cos(x)
> \end{align}
> $$
