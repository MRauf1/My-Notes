# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Absorption Laws)
> For [[Proposition|statements]] $P, Q$[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P \lor (P \land Q) = P \\
> P \land (P \lor Q) = P
> \end{align}
> $$


[^1]: [HOW TO PROVE IT: A Structured Approach, Second Edition](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/THI2Q4PN?page=35)
