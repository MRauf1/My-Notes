# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Laurent Series]] Representation)
> If $f$ is [[Complex Analytic Function]] on a ring $A = \{z | R_1 < |z - z_0| < R_2\}$, then $f$ has a [[Laurent Series]] representation on $A$. The [[Ring of Convergence]] of the [[Laurent Series]] contains $A$.
