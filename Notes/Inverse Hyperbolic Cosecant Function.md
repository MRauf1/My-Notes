# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Inverse Function]] [[Hyperbolic Cosecant Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> csch^{-1}(x) = \ln(\frac{1}{x} + \sqrt{\frac{1}{x^2} + 1})
> \end{align}
> $$
> [[Domain]] is $\mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}$ and [[Range]] is $\mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}$.
