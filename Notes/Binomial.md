# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Binomial)[^1]
> $\forall x_1, a_1, b_1, x_2, a_2, b_2$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> a_1 x_1^{b_1} + a_2 x_2^{b_2}
> \end{align}
> $$

Binomial is a [[Polynomial|polynomial]] with $2$ [[Term|terms]].
