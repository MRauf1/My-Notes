# Definition


> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Random Walk]] with [[Drift]])
> $X_t = \delta + X_{t-1} + W_t$
> With initial condition $x_0 = 0$, $X_t = \delta t + \sum_{j=1}^{t} W_j$

Random Walk: value at time $t$ is the value of series at time $t-1$ plus a completely random movement determined by $W_t$
Drift: $\delta$
