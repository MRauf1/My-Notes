# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Plane Equation)[^1]
> Given 2 [[Point]] on the plane $\mathbf{p}_1, \mathbf{p}_2$ and [[Normal Vector]] to the plane $\mathbf{n}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \mathbf{n} \cdot (\mathbf{p}_1 - \mathbf{p}_2) = 0
> \end{align}
> $$
