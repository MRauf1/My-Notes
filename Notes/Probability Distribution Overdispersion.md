# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Probability Distribution]] Overdispersion)[^1]
> When a [[Probability Distribution]] exhibits greater [[Variance]] than predicted by the distribution.
> $$
> \begin{align}
> E[X] &= E[E[X | \mu]] \\
> Var[X] &= E[Var[X | \mu]] + Var[E[X | \mu]]
> \end{align}
> $$
