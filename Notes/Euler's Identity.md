# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Leonhard Euler]] Identity)
> $$
> \begin{align}
> e^{i \theta} = \cos(\theta) + i \sin(\theta)
> \end{align}
> $$

# Properties
- $z = r e^{i \theta}$ for a [[Complex Number]]
- $e^{i \theta_1} e^{i \theta_2} = e^{i (\theta_1 + \theta_2)}$
- $|e^{i \theta}| = 1$
- $\overline{e^{i \theta}} = e^{- i \theta} = (e^{i \theta})^{-1}$
