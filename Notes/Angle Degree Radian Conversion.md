# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Degree to Radian)[^1]
> To convert from degrees $x_d$ to radians $x_r$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> x_r = x_d \cdot \frac{\pi}{180}
> \end{align}
> $$

> [!abstract] Theorem 2 (Radian to Degree)[^1]
> To convert from radians $x_r$ to degrees $x_d$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> x_d = x_r \cdot \frac{180}{\pi}
> \end{align}
> $$
