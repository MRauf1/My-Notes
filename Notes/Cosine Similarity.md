# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Cosine Function]] Similarity)
> Cosine similarity of two [[Vector]] $\mathbf{a}, \mathbf{b}$ is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \cos(\theta) = \frac{\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{b}}{||\mathbf{a}|| ||\mathbf{b}||}
> \end{align}
> $$
> Its [[Range]] is $[-1, 1]$.

Measures the extent to which the two [[Vector]] point in the same direction. If $\pm 1$, they are [[Parallel Vector]]. If $0$, then they are [[Orthogonal Vector]].
