# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Universal Quantifier]])
> With regards to [[Conditional Statement]], the following two are equivalent:
1) $x \in A \implies P(x)$
2) $\forall x \in A, P(x)$

> [!abstract] Theorem 2 ([[Existential Quantifier]])
> With regards to [[Conjunction Argument]], the following two are equivalent:
1) $\exists x \in A, P(x)$
2) $x \in A \land P(x)$
