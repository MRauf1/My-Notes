# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Proportion)[^1]
> For $b \neq 0, d \neq 0$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d}
> \end{align}
> $$

Proportion is an [[Equation]] of two [[Ratio|ratios]] or [[Rate|rates]]. It is read as "$a$ is to $b$, as $c$ is to $d$".

> [!example]- Examples 1 (Proportion)
> > [!example]- Example 1.1 (Simple Proportion)
> > $\frac{2}{4} = \frac{4}{8}$
