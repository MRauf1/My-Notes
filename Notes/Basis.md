# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Basis)
> Basis of [[Group]] $G$ is a [[Subset]] $B \subseteq G$ which is [[Linearly Independent]] and generates $G$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> G = <B> = \mathbb{Z}B = \{c_1 x_1 + \dots c_n x_n | c_i \in \mathbb{Z}, x_i \in B\}
> \end{align}
> $$
