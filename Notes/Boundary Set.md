# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Boundary Set)[^1]
> Boundary of [[Set]] $E$ is the [[Set]] $E^- \setminus E^{\circ}$.
> [[Point]] in this [[Set]] are called boundary points.

Boundary of $[a, b]$ and $(a, b)$ is both $\{a, b\}$.
