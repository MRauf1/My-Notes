# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Cauchy's Inequality)
> If $f$ is [[Complex Analytic Function]] on $B(z_0, R)$, then for all $r \in (0, R)$, for all $k \in \mathbb{N}$,
> $$
> \begin{align}
> |f^{(k)}(z_0)| \leq \frac{k!}{r^k} M_r
> \end{align}
> $$
> where $M_r = \max_{|z - z_0| = r} |f(z)|$.
