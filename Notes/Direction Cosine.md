# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Direction Cosine)[^1]
> For a [[Vector]] $\mathbf{a}$, the [[Angle]] it makes with the $x_i$ axis of the coordinate system is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> cos(\theta_i) = \frac{\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{e}_i}{||\mathbf{a}|| ||\mathbf{e}_i||} = \frac{a_i}{||\mathbf{a}||}
> \end{align}
> $$
> This value is the direction cosine and the angle is the direction angle.

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=842)
