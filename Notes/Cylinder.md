# Definition

Cylinder is a solid figure with $2$ parallel [[Circle|circles]] with radius $r$ on top and bottom, known as bases. The height $h$ is the [[Distance|distance]] between the bases.[^1]

![[Cylinder.png]]

# Properties

## [[Surface Area|Surface Area]]

$$S = 2 \pi r^2 + 2 \pi r h$$

## [[Volume|Volume]]

$$V = \pi r^2 h$$
