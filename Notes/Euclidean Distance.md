# Definition

> [!info] Definition
> For two $n$-dimensional [[Vector|vectors]] $\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{y}$, the Euclidean [[Distance|distance]] between them is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> d(\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{y}) = ||\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y}|| = \sqrt{(x_1 - y_1)^2 + (x_2 - y_2)^2 + ... + (x_n - y_n)^2}
> \end{align}
> $$
