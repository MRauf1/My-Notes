# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Logarithm]] Product Rule)[^1]
> For a fixed base $b \in \mathbb{R}^+ \setminus \{1\}$, and $u, w \in \mathbb{R}^+$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> log_b(uw) = log_b(u) + log_b(w)
> \end{align}
> $$
