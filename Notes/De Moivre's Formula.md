# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (De Moivre's Formula)
> $$
> \begin{align}
> (\cos(\theta) + i \sin(\theta))^n = (e^{i \theta})^n = \cos(n \theta) + i \sin(n \theta)
> \end{align}
> $$

> [!info] Definition 2 ([[Nth Root]] of [[Complex Number]])
> Given a [[Complex Number]] $z$, its [[Nth Root]]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> z^{\frac{1}{n}} = r^{\frac{1}{n}} (\cos(\frac{\theta + 2 \pi k}{n}) + i \sin(\frac{\theta + 2 \pi k}{n}))
> \end{align}
> $$
> for $k = 0, \dots, n - 1$
