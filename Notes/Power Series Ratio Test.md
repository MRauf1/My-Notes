# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Power Series]] [[Infinite Series Ratio Test]])[^1]
> For [[Power Series]] $\sum a_n (x - a)^n$, let $\beta = \lim |\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}|$ and $R = \frac{1}{\beta}$. If $\beta = 0$, then we set $R = + \infty$, and if $\beta = + \infty$, we set $R = 0$.  Then
> 1) Power series [[Absolutely Convergent Infinite Series|converges absolutely]] for $|x - a| < R$
> 2) Power series diverges for $|x - a| > R$
> 3) Power series may or may not converge/diverge at the endpoints

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=199)
