# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Factor Theorem)[^1]
> Suppose $p(x)$ is a non-zero polynomial function. $c \in \mathbb{R} \lor c \in \mathbb{C}$ is a zero of $p$ $\iff$ $(x - c)$ is a [[Factor|factor]] of $p(x)$
