# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Unbounded Sequence]] [[Decreasing Sequence]] [[Limit of Sequence]])[^1]
> If $(s_n)$ is unbounded decreasing sequence, then $lim(s_n) = -\infty$.
