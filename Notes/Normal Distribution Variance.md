# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Normal Distribution]] [[Variance]])[^1]
> For a normal distribution $N(\mu, \sigma^2)$, its variance is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> Var[X] = \sigma^2
> \end{align}
> $$
