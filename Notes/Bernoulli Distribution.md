# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Bernoulli [[Probability Distribution]])[^1]
> Denoted as $Bernoulli(p)$
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P(x) &= \begin{cases}p & x = 1\\ 1 - p & x = 0\end{cases} = p^x(1 - p)^{1 - x}
> \end{align}
> $$

Distribution for a single binary observation (e.g. success or failure).
