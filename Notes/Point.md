# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Point)[^1]
> Point in n-dimensional space is an ordered tuple $\mathbf{p} = (p_1, \dots, p_n) \in \mathbb{R}^n$ (if in real space).

Point $(0, \dots, 0)$ is called the [[Origin]] of $\mathbb{R}^n$.
