# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Line Integral Independent of Path]] for [[Closed Curve]])[^1]
> [[Line Integral of Vector Field]] $\int_C V \cdot d\mathbf{r}$ is independent of path in $D$ if and only if $\int_C V \cdot d\mathbf{r} = 0$ for every [[Closed Curve]] $C$ in $D$

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=1121)
