# Definition

Attachment refers to the bond between the child and caregiver.[^1]

Research conducted by [[John Bowlby]] and [[Mary Ainsworth]].

Received some criticisms for lack of control for culture, and criticism pointed out that child's temperament may have an effect on attachment.

Heavily influences one's view/expectations of future significant relationships and remains stable throughout lifespan. However, someone with an insecure attachment can re-approach attachment quality by forming a significant relationship with a securely attached person at any time in life.
