# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Cosecant [[Function]])[^1]
> Cosecant function is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \csc(\theta) = \frac{1}{\sin(\theta)}
> \end{align}
> $$
> provided $\sin(\theta) \neq 0$.

Has [[Domain]] $\{\theta | \theta \neq \pi k, k \in \mathbb{Z}\}$ and [[Range]] $(-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty)$.
