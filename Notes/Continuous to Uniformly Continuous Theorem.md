# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 ([[Continuous Function]] to [[Uniformly Continuous Function]] Theorem)[^1]
> If $f$ is continuous on a closed interval $[a, b]$, then it is [[Uniformly Continuous Function]] on $[a, b]$.
> Alternatively, $f$ is continuous on a [[Closed Set]] and [[Bounded Set]] $S$, then it is uniformly continuous on $S$.

[^1]: [Elementary Analysis: The Theory of Calculus](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GUY2WR3V?page=154)
