# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Probability Function|Probability]] Mass Function)[^1]
> For a [[Discrete Random Variable|discrete RV]] $X$, its [[Probability|probability]] $P(X = x)$ denoted by $f(x)$ is the pmf of $X$.
