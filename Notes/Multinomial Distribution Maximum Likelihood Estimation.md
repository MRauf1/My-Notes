# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Multinomial Distribution]] [[Maximum Likelihood Estimation]])
> For a $Multinomial(n, p_1, \dots, p_c)$ distribution, its MLE is
> $$
> \begin{align}
> L(\mathbf{p}) &= \sum_{j} n_j ln(p_j) \\
> \hat{p_j} &= \frac{n_j}{n}
> \end{align}
> $$
