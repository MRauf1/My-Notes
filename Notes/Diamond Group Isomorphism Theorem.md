# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Diamond [[Group Isomorphism]] Theorem)
> For a [[Group]] $G$ with $A \leq G, N \trianglelefteq G$
> 1) $AN = NA$
> 2) $AN \leq G$ with $N \trianglelefteq AN$
> 3) $A \cap N \trianglelefteq A$
> 4) $AN/N \cong A/A \cap N$
