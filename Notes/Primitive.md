# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Primitive)
> Let $D \subseteq \mathbb{C}$ be [[Open Set]] and $f: D \rightarrow \mathbb{C}$ be [[Continuous Function]]. We say $F$ is a primitive of $f$ on $D$ if $F$ is [[Analytic Function]] on $D$ and $F' = f$ on $D$.
