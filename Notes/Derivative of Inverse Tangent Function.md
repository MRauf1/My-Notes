# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 ([[Derivative]] of [[Inverse Tangent Function]])[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \frac{d}{dx}(\arctan(x)) = \frac{1}{1 + x^2}
> \end{align}
> $$
