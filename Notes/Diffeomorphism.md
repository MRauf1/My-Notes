# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Diffeomorphism)[^1]
> [[Mapping]] that has a ([[Smooth Function]]) [[Inverse Function|inverse]] [[Mapping]] is called a diffeomorphism.
> It is a type of [[Regular Mapping]].
