# Definition

> [!abstract] Theorem 1 (Morera's Theorem)
> If $f$ is [[Continuous Function]] on [[Open Set]] $D \subseteq \mathbb{C}$ and $\int_{\partial \triangle} f = 0$ for all triangles contained in $D$, then $f$ is [[Complex Analytic Function]] on $D$.
