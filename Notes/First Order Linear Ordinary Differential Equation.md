# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Linear Equation)[^1]
> [[First Order Ordinary Differential Equation]] of the form $\frac{dy}{dx} + P(x) y = Q(x)$,
> 1) Multiply both sides by the [[Integrating Factor]] $I(x) = e^{\int P(x) dx}$
> 2) The resulting equation becomes $\frac{d}{dx}(I(x) y) = I(x) Q(x)$
> 3) Integrate both sides

[^1]: [Calculus: Early Transcendentals](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/EEFDQ9Y5?page=653)
