# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Exterior [[Set]])
> Given a [[Set]] $S$, the exterior $S^e$ of set $S$ is the set of all [[Point]] that are outside of $S$ ([[Interior Set]]) and its [[Boundary Set]]. It is the [[Interior Set]] of the [[Set Complement]] of $S$ or the [[Set Complement]] of the [[Closure Set]] of $S$.
> $$
> \begin{align}
> S^e = (S^c)^{\circ} = (S^-)^c
> \end{align}
> $$
