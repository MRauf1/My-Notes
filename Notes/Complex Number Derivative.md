# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Derivative of [[Complex Number]])
> For $z \in \mathbb{C}$ and $f(z) = u(z) + i\ v(z)$. If $f$ is differentiable at $z_0 = x_0 + i\ y_0$, then $u, v$ have [[Partial Derivative]] at $z_0$ and
> $$
> \begin{align}
> f'(z_0) = u_x(z_0) + i\ v_x(z_0) = v_y(z_0) - i\ u_y(z_0)
> \end{align}
> $$
