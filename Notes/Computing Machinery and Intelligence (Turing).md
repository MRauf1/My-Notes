# Review[^1]

This is the paper that introduces the famous Imitation Game. The game pits an AI and a human, where the AI has to get the interrogator to incorrectly state that the AI is a human, while the human tries to get the interrogator to correctly identify the human as a human. The interrogator knows that one is a human and one is an AI and has to correctly distinguish which one is which.

Turing recognized that AI may think differently from how humans think even if both AI and humans are intelligent.

[^1]: [Computing Machinery and Intelligence](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PGZER5NA?page=1)
