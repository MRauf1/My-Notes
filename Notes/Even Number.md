# Definition

> [!info] Definition 1 (Even [[Number|Number]])
> [[Integer|Integer]] $x$ is even if[^1]
> $$
> \begin{align}
> \exists k \in \mathbb{Z}, x = 2k
> \end{align}
> $$

[^1]: [HOW TO PROVE IT: A Structured Approach, Second Edition](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/THI2Q4PN?page=140)
